Find Your Relax in Our Region

Founded in 1994, Areni Wine Factory is among the oldest and most well-established wine facilities in the area. The winery has welcomed some 30,000 curious (and thirsty!) visitors every year since 2012. A tour of the factory includes a walk through the cellar where wine is aged in massive oak casks, followed by a tasting of the winery’s selection of traditional and fruit wines.

In an upstairs dining area, guests can order coffee or tea and watch a short video about the Armenian winemaking tradition and the history of the company. Lunch and lavash making master class on site can be arranged with advance noticed. A small craft shop offers souvenirs and handmade gifts as well as a selection of wines for purchase.
